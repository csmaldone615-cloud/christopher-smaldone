04 · How I Sell
01
Discovery-First, Always
I don't pitch until I've diagnosed. Every deal starts with deep discovery — understanding the business impact of pain, who owns it, and what success looks like to each stakeholder. MEDDICC isn't a checklist; it's how I think.
02
Multi-Threading is Non-Negotiable
Single-threaded deals die. I map every account across Champion, Economic Buyer, and Technical Stakeholder — using digital rooms, Salesloft sequences, and exec alignment to build depth before I need it.
03
Business Case Over Feature Selling
By the time I'm closing, the buyer has already built the ROI case in their head — because I helped them build it. I develop custom cases tied to the metrics each stakeholder cares about: pipeline velocity, rep productivity, CFO visibility.
04
Long-Game Account Management
Winning the deal is the beginning, not the end. I drive adoption, measure outcomes, and turn customers into internal champions. Expansion is the natural result of value delivered — not a separate motion.
